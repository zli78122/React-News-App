## Functionalities
	1. search by section
	2. search by keyword & Auto Suggestion
	3. bookmark
	4. article source switch between Guardian News and New York Times
	5. share article to Facebook, Twitter, and Email
	6. article details
	7. comment
	8. adapt to both PC and mobile
